## Key Patterns

- **AI/ML is a recurring differentiator** — Medi, DermaSpect, AdaptEd, DOC, and the migrant worker legal tool all used AI as a core component.
- **Platform/marketplace models do well** — Ethical Microloans, BagSHARE, and the second-hand clothing app are all platform plays connecting supply and demand.
- **Accessibility for underserved populations** is a constant winning theme — refugees, rural doctors, migrant workers, people with learning disabilities.
- **Simple, focused solutions win** — none of these are overly complex. They identify one clear problem and build a targeted tool.
- **Web apps and mobile apps dominate** — most winners built app-based solutions, which aligns well with PWA development skills.
