Each hackathon project will be built around a specific theme - a focus area that outlines the kind of problems you’ll be solving and the impact your solution aims to create. Themes are designed to help guide your creativity, give structure to your ideas, and ensure your project is aligned with the overall goals of the event.

Your final project will be evaluated by a panel of judges who are experts in that theme’s domain. A strong alignment between your project and your chosen theme can significantly boost your score, as it shows you’ve understood the problem space, used relevant insights, and tailored your solution to the needs and opportunities in that area.

In short:

- Themes help you focus your ideas.
- Judges are specialists in the theme you choose.
- The closer your solution matches the theme’s goals, the better your chances of impressing the panel.

# Overview

‹#›

How can we future-proof the environment for everyone? And how can we urgently respond to the important challenges already occurring?

Coastal communities will need to contend with sea level rise and severe storms while the interior of continents will see more weather extremes - both hot and cold, wet and dry.
