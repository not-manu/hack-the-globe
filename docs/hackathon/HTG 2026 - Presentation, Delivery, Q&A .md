**Judging Rubric: Presentation, Delivery, Q\&A**  
*Hack The Globe 2026*

| Criteria | 3/10 | 5/10 | 7/10 | 10/10 |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Presentation, Delivery and Q\&A ** | Disorganized and unclear presentation with weak link between problem and solution. Delivery is hesitant and unengaging; heavy reliance on notes.  Responses to questions are vague or show limited understanding of the problem and solution.   | Basic structure and understandable delivery, but lacks flow and audience connection.  Responses to questions show partial understanding of the problem and solution, with limited clarity or justification.  | Clear and structured presentation with mostly smooth delivery. Engaging overall, though minor lapses in pacing or clarity.  Responses to questions are clear but may lack depth or nuance.  | Highly organized, compelling, and engaging presentation. Confident delivery, and strong audience engagement.  Responses to questions are clear, well-reasoned, and demonstrate deep understanding.  |
